About

When people hear the words 'library and information science', they mostly associate it with related words such as libraries, librarian, books, archives, etc. While this may be the case, it is not always true, especially now in the advent of technology. In order to break this stigma, I, as a student currently under LIS 51, aim to properly inform people about library and information science as a field, the importance of such field, the different professions that one may acquire in the said field, and debunk the misconceptions surrounding it.

Objectives

  • To inform the public about LIS
  • To debunk the misconceptions surrounding LIS
  • To propagate the truth regarding LIS
